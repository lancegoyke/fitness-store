# Template 101 program grid (Base 1) — new freeform format

Banner (merged): "Weekly Schedule - 3x resistance - 1x metabolic - 2-3x easy aerobic"
Header: | Day N | Exercise | Tempo | Week 1 | Week 2 | Week 3 | Week 4 | Coach Comments | Athlete Comments | Rest |
Weeks are 4 SIDE-BY-SIDE columns. Progression = rep/set change per week column; RPE on its own sub-row. NO load column, NO load in cells.
